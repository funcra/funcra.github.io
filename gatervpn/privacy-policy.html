Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 1, 2025

Introduction

GaterVPN ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our mobile application (the "App").

Important Notice: GaterVPN is a client application only. We do not provide VPN servers. The security of VPN connections established through this App depends entirely on the VPN Gate servers you connect to, not on this client application. We cannot guarantee the security, privacy, or logging practices of VPN Gate servers. Please read the "VPN Connection Security" and "VPN Gate Disclaimer and Limitations" sections below for complete details.

Information We Collect

Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be transparent about what we do not collect:

  • No Personal Information: We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or physical address.
  • No VPN Connection Logs (Client-Side): As the client application developer, we do not log, monitor, or store your VPN connection activities, browsing history, or any data transmitted through the VPN connection. However, please note that VPN Gate servers (which we do not control) may maintain their own logs. The security and logging practices of VPN Gate servers are entirely controlled by VPN Gate and individual server providers, not by this App.
  • No Location Data: We do not collect or track your location information.
  • No User Content (Client-Side): As the client application, we do not access, collect, or store any content you access through the VPN connection. All VPN traffic is handled directly between your device and the VPN Gate server. We have no visibility into or control over the data transmitted through VPN connections.

Information We Collect

1. Local App Data (Stored on Your Device Only)

The following data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party:

  • VPN Server Information: Server lists, connection history, and favorite servers are stored locally in an embedded database on your device.
  • App Preferences: Your app settings and preferences are stored locally using iOS UserDefaults.
  • Connection Statistics: Connection count and usage statistics are stored locally for the purpose of displaying ads in the free version.

2. Advertising Data (Google AdMob)

Our App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements in the free version. AdMob may collect and use the following information:

  • Advertising ID: AdMob may collect your device's advertising identifier (IDFA) to serve personalized ads.
  • Device Information: AdMob may collect device information such as device type, operating system version, and language settings.
  • Usage Data: AdMob may collect information about how you interact with advertisements.

For more information about how Google uses data, please visit: Google's Privacy Policy

You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device's advertising settings:

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy & Security > Apple Advertising > Personalized Ads

3. In-App Purchase Data

When you make an in-app purchase, the transaction is processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Apple may collect and process payment information according to their privacy policy.

How We Use Information

Local Data

  • Server Management: We use locally stored server information to provide VPN connection functionality.
  • User Experience: We use locally stored preferences and history to improve your app experience.
  • Ad Display: We use connection count data to determine when to display interstitial ads in the free version.

Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob: We use AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob's use of information is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • VPN Gate Services: When you connect to a VPN server, your connection is established directly between your device and the VPN Gate server. As the client application developer, we do not intercept, monitor, or log any data transmitted through the VPN connection. However, the security, privacy practices, and potential logging of VPN Gate servers are entirely controlled by VPN Gate and individual server providers, not by this App.
  • App Store: In-app purchases are processed by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. However, we use the following third-party services that may collect information:

  1. Google AdMob: For advertising services. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.
  2. Apple App Store: For in-app purchase processing. See Apple's Privacy Policy for details.
  3. VPN Gate: VPN connections are established directly between your device and VPN Gate servers. We are not involved in the data transmission. VPN Gate servers may maintain their own logs and have their own privacy practices, which we do not control.

Data Security

Client-Side Security

  • All data stored locally on your device is protected by iOS security features.
  • We do not transmit any user data to our servers (we do not maintain any servers).
  • We do not have access to any data transmitted through VPN connections.

VPN Connection Security

Important: GaterVPN is a client application only. We do not provide VPN servers. The security of your VPN connections depends entirely on:

  • VPN Gate Servers: VPN connections are established directly with VPN Gate servers, which are provided by volunteers worldwide. The security, encryption, and privacy practices of these servers are controlled by VPN Gate and individual server operators, not by this App.
  • OpenVPN Protocol: While VPN connections use the OpenVPN protocol for encryption, the actual security implementation depends on the VPN Gate server configuration, which we do not control.
  • Server Logging: VPN Gate servers may maintain their own connection logs. We have no control over or visibility into what VPN Gate servers log or how they handle your data.

We cannot guarantee the security or privacy of VPN Gate servers. By using this App, you acknowledge that VPN communication security depends on VPN Gate servers, not on this client application.

Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings.
  • Premium Version: You can purchase the premium version to remove all advertisements.

Data Retention

  • Local Data: All data stored locally on your device remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the App.
  • Advertising Data: AdMob's data retention is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • No Server-Side Data: We do not maintain any servers that store your data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App and updating the "Last Updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

Third-Party Links and Services

Our App may contain links to third-party websites or services (such as VPN Gate). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third parties. We encourage you to read their privacy policies.

GaterVPN is an unofficial client application for VPN Gate services. We do not provide VPN servers.

VPN Gate is a research project of the Graduate School of University of Tsukuba, Japan. When you use VPN Gate servers through this App:

  • We are a client only: This App is merely a client interface for connecting to VPN Gate servers. We do not operate, control, or maintain any VPN servers.
  • Direct connection: Your VPN connection is established directly between your device and the VPN Gate server. We are not involved in the data transmission.
  • No control over servers: We have no access to, control over, or responsibility for:
    • VPN Gate server security practices
    • VPN Gate server logging policies
    • VPN Gate server privacy practices
    • VPN Gate server data handling
    • VPN Gate server availability or reliability
  • Server security: The security of your VPN connections depends entirely on the VPN Gate server you connect to. Each server is operated by volunteers, and security practices may vary.
  • No guarantees: We cannot and do not guarantee:
    • The security of VPN Gate servers
    • That VPN Gate servers do not log your activities
    • That VPN Gate servers protect your privacy
    • The reliability or availability of VPN Gate servers
  • Your responsibility: By using this App, you acknowledge that:
    • VPN communication security depends on VPN Gate servers, not on this client application
    • You use VPN Gate servers at your own risk
    • We are not responsible for any security issues, data breaches, or privacy violations that may occur on VPN Gate servers

For more information about VPN Gate, please visit: VPN Gate Website
